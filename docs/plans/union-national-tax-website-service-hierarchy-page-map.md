# Union National Tax — Website Service Hierarchy and Page Map

## Purpose

This document defines the actual website service hierarchy and page map for Union National Tax.

The goal is to keep the site broad enough for business owners in general while still letting the firm's strongest offers and niche credibility stand out.

The site should reflect an **advisory-led positioning** where strategy comes first, financial control comes next, specialization strengthens trust, and compliance support sits underneath.

---

# Website Service Hierarchy

## Tier 1 — Primary Revenue and Positioning Offers

These are the offers that should lead the site.

### 1. S-Corp Tax Strategy
This is one of the clearest front-end offers and one of the easiest for owners to understand.

Core themes:
- tax savings opportunity
- entity structure
- salary vs. distributions
- owner compensation planning
- strategic entry into deeper advisory work

### 2. Fractional CFO Services
This is the flagship strategic offer.

Core themes:
- financial clarity
- cash flow forecasting
- KPI dashboards
- monthly reporting
- strategic decision support
- profitability improvement

### 3. Tax Planning Consulting
This is the proactive, year-round strategy offer for owners who need tax planning but are not yet ready for a full CFO retainer.

Core themes:
- quarterly planning
- proactive savings strategies
- entity review
- estimated tax planning
- growth-stage guidance

### 4. Industry-Specific CFO Partnerships
These are premium, verticalized offers that make Union National Tax feel specialized and operationally relevant rather than generic.

Current public verticals:
- Construction
- Restaurants

---

## Tier 2 — Supporting Delivery Offers

These support the core strategy offers.

### 5. Strategic Bookkeeping
This should be positioned as the financial visibility layer that supports better decisions and clean reporting.

Core themes:
- cleaner books
- reconciliations
- reporting accuracy
- tax-ready financials
- better financial visibility

### 6. Tax Filing & Preparation
This belongs on the site, but as supporting compliance rather than the lead identity of the brand.

Core themes:
- reliable filing support
- individual and business-owner returns
- clean execution
- support within the broader advisory relationship

---

## Tier 3 — Entry / Implementation Offers

These are important but should not dominate the main positioning.

### 7. New Business Formation
This is a useful implementation offer for startups and early-stage owners.

Core themes:
- LLC formation
- S-Corp setup
- C-Corp setup
- EIN support
- BOI compliance guidance

### 8. Wealth Architecture / Advanced Strategy
This is the higher-level advisory lane for owners who want more advanced planning.

Core themes:
- deeper tax optimization
- long-term wealth strategy
- investor-minded planning
- entity design
- advanced proactive guidance

# Strategic Takeaway

The site should not feel like:

**“Here are 12 accounting services.”**

It should feel like:

**“We help business owners reduce taxes, gain financial control, and build smarter companies through strategy-first advisory.”**

That is the practical website service hierarchy and page-map version of the broader offer architecture.
