# Union National Tax — Website Offer Architecture

## Core Positioning

Union National Tax should be presented as a **boutique advisory-led tax strategy and CFO firm for growth-minded business owners**, not as a tax prep shop.

Core message:

**Stop overpaying the IRS. Build a smarter business.**

## Offer Ladder

### 1. Front-End Entry Offers

These are the easiest ways for a business owner to enter the ecosystem.

#### S-Corp Tax Strategy / S-Corp Tax Advantage Program
This should be a signature entry point.

Why it matters:
- concrete
- high-value
- easy to explain
- directly tied to tax savings
- a strong doorway into deeper advisory work

This offer connects directly to:
- S-Corp elections
- entity optimization
- owner compensation planning
- tax savings strategy
- deeper advisory upsell

#### Tax Planning Consulting
This is the proactive planning door for owners who may not need full CFO support yet, but do need year-round tax strategy and quarterly review.

#### New Business Formation
This is a lower-ticket implementation offer that can feed into S-Corp and advisory services.

Includes:
- LLC setup
- S-Corp setup
- C-Corp setup
- EIN support
- BOI compliance guidance

Positioning note:
This should not be the hero offer, but it belongs on the site as an action-oriented entry point.

### 2. Core Recurring Advisory Offer

This is the real flagship.

#### Fractional CFO Services
This is what elevates the firm beyond compliance.

Core outcomes:
- financial clarity
- cash flow forecasting
- budgeting
- KPI visibility
- monthly reporting
- decision support
- profit improvement
- wealth acceleration

Positioning note:
This should be framed as the core strategic partnership offer, not just an add-on service.

### 3. Supporting Implementation Layer

These are not the main identity of the firm, but they support retention and service delivery.

#### Strategic Bookkeeping
This should be framed as decision-support bookkeeping, not commodity bookkeeping.

Core outcomes:
- cleaner books
- reporting accuracy
- better business decisions
- stronger tax readiness
- financial visibility

#### Tax Filing & Preparation
This belongs on the site, but only as supporting compliance.

Positioning note:
Tax prep should be framed as reliable execution support, not the primary identity of the brand.

### 4. Premium Verticalized Offers

These are specialized offers that make Union National Tax feel more relevant and differentiated.

#### Construction CFO Partnership
A hybrid CFO + COO style partnership focused on:
- job costing automation
- real-time margin tracking
- cash flow visibility
- project financial control
- estimating and profitability discipline

#### Restaurant CFO Partnership
A restaurant-specific advisory partnership focused on:
- inventory and COGS control
- labor discipline
- margin visibility
- menu pricing strategy
- operational financial control

### 5. Wealth-Building / Advanced Strategy Layer

This is the upper-end advisory theme.

#### Wealth Architecture / Advanced Tax Strategy
This supports higher-level clients who want:
- deeper tax optimization
- entity strategy
- long-term wealth planning
- investor-style tax thinking
- proactive financial design beyond compliance

## Best Website Structure

For the public website, simplify everything into **three main doors**:

### Door 1: Reduce Taxes
This is where the following live:
- S-Corp Strategy
- Tax Planning Consulting
- Entity Optimization
- New Business Formation

### Door 2: Gain Financial Control
This is where the following live:
- Fractional CFO Services
- Strategic Bookkeeping
- Cash flow support
- KPI guidance
- Profitability support

### Door 3: Industry-Specific Advisory
This is where the following live:
- Construction CFO Partnership
- Restaurant CFO Partnership
- future niche-specific partnerships

## Supporting Service Placement

Keep **Tax Preparation** as a supporting service under the broader Services page, not as the headline offer.

That preserves the brand’s premium advisory position.

## Recommended Homepage Structure

### Hero
**Stop overpaying the IRS. Build a smarter business.**

### Three Core Paths
- Tax Strategy
- CFO Advisory
- Industry Partnerships

### Primary CTA
**Book Your Discovery Evaluation**

### Secondary CTA
**Take the Proactive CFO Assessment**

## Recommended Conversion Path

The website should guide users through this path:

**Book / lead magnet / authority content**  
→ **Discovery Evaluation or Proactive CFO Assessment**  
→ **S-Corp Strategy or Tax Planning offer**  
→ **Bookkeeping + CFO retainer**  
→ **Verticalized partnership or wealth architecture engagement**

## Strategic Summary

Union National Tax should not present itself as a long menu of unrelated services.

It should present itself as **one advisory-led system** with three main entry points:

- Tax Strategy
- CFO Advisory
- Industry Partnerships

Everything else should support that structure.

That keeps the brand:
- premium
- clear
- advisory-led
- specialized
- more valuable than a generic tax prep or bookkeeping firm
