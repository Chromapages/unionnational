# Union National Tax — Sitemap

## Document Purpose
This sitemap defines the recommended information architecture for the next version of the Union National Tax website. It translates the approved positioning, client targeting, messaging, offer hierarchy, and website PRD into a practical page structure.

The goal is to keep the website aligned to Union National Tax’s strategy-first positioning, with S-Corp strategy, proactive tax planning, and Fractional CFO support leading the narrative rather than generic tax-prep messaging.

---

## 1. Sitemap Objectives

This sitemap is designed to:

- position Union National Tax as a boutique tax strategy and financial advisory firm
- lead with high-value advisory offers instead of commodity-first services
- support qualified strategy-call bookings
- create clear paths for best-fit business owners
- support current specialized programs in construction and restaurants
- leave room for future vertical expansion and content growth

---

## 2. Architecture Principles

### 2.1 Advisory-first structure
Core architecture should lead with strategy and outcomes:
- S-Corp Tax Advantage Program
- Fractional CFO Services
- Tax Planning Consulting

### 2.2 Supporting services remain visible but secondary
These services should be easy to find, but they should not dominate the navigation or homepage narrative:
- Strategic Bookkeeping
- Tax Filing & Preparation
- New Business Formation

### 2.3 Industry specialization should be visible
Construction and Restaurants should have dedicated pages because they are active specialized offers.

### 2.4 Conversion paths should be explicit
The sitemap should push users toward:
- Book a Tax Strategy Call
- See If an S-Corp Could Save You Money
- Learn about the best-fit service

## 10. Strategic Notes

### What this sitemap intentionally avoids
- making Tax Filing & Preparation the hero service
- presenting bookkeeping as a commodity standalone identity
- using generic “Contact Us” as the primary site action
- overcrowding the first release with too many industries
- overbuilding content before service architecture is clear

### What this sitemap intentionally supports
- premium advisory perception
- S-Corp-led acquisition
- industry specialization
- future SEO and funnel expansion
- stronger booking behavior from qualified business owners

## 11. Final Recommendation

The first live sitemap should stay focused, conversion-oriented, and advisory-led.

The cleanest first-release structure is:

- Home
- Services
- Industries
- About
- FAQ
- Book a Strategy Call

With service pages centered on:
- S-Corp Tax Advantage Program
- Fractional CFO Services
- Tax Planning Consulting
- Strategic Bookkeeping
- Tax Filing & Preparation
- New Business Formation

And industry pages centered on:
- Construction
- Restaurants

This gives Union National Tax a site structure that matches the business it is trying to become publicly, while still reflecting the real services it currently delivers.
