Common Questions

Is this suitable for a brand new LLC?
Yes. In fact, setting this up from day one is ideal. The book covers how to file your initial election (Form 2553) within the 75-day window for new businesses.
Do I need an accountant if I buy this book?
This book is designed to let you "DIY" the setup and maintenance. However, we always recommend having a CPA file your actual year-end tax return. This book handles the payroll, reasonable comp, and bookkeeping systems that CPAs often charge thousands to manage.
What if I make less than $60k?
The book explains why $60k is typically the "break-even" point. If you earn less, the administrative costs of payroll (Gusto fees, etc.) might outweigh the tax savings. The book helps you calculate your specific number.
